First-class biomedical engineer (KNUST) working where AI meets medicine.

Robert Afiakwa Owusu

Engineer · AI Researcher · Educator

I graduated top of my class in Biomedical Engineering at KNUST (First Class, CWA 84.07/100), and was named Valedictorian of the Department of Computer Engineering. My focus now is AI in tissue engineering and computational medicine, with an active thread in AI for medical imaging.

I currently work as an AI Engineer in computational medicine with the Global Health AI & Compute Lab (GHAIC-L) at KCCR, and I teach and tutor alongside my research. I care about building accessible, low-cost technology for under-resourced settings — and about bringing people along with me.
